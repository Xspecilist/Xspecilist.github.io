Assisi Convent School

Assisi Convent school, Noida , is a private un-aided Christian minority Institution established in 1987 by the Sisters of the Franciscan Clarist Congregation under the administration of Noida Assisi sekchik sanstha of the San Damiano Province. It aims at importing an education which will bring about the intellectual, social, aesthetic, spiritual and physical growth of the child's personality.
